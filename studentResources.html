Student Resources

Definition Of Taekwondo

Taekwondo is an unarmed Korean Martial Art. It is not only kicking and punching but a way of life. The ultimate goal of Taekwondo is to achieve full control of body and mind.

School Rules


1. Students must bow to the flags suh bum nim and other black belt instructors when coming in and out of class.

2.Students must use the words sir or maam when speaking to instructors.

3. Students must respect and obey instructors.

4. Conversations are not permitted during class.

5. Swearing, smoking, and drinking liquor are prohibited in the do jang.

6. Students must come to class regularly for their own good

7. Students must help keep the dojang clean

The Spirit of TKD


1.Loyalty to our country.

2.Obedience to our Parents.

3.Respect our Elders.

4.Be Faithful to our Friends.

5.Be Courteous in all Situations.

6.Consider all alternatives before killing any living thing.

7.Never give up: persevere to finish what you start.

Membership Oath


1.We as members, train our spirits and bodies, according to the strict code.

2.We as members, are united in mutual friendship.

3.We as members, will comply with regulations and obey instructors.
